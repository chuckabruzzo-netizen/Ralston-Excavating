About Ralston Excavating

Ralston Excavating is a local, family-run outfit based in Colton, Oregon, focused on practical septic and excavation work for homes, small farms, and light commercial projects in the surrounding communities.

Instead of sending a rotating crew, the same owner‑operator shows up to walk the site, run the machine, and keep you in the loop from first phone call to final backfill. The goal on every job is simple: leave you with a solid system, good drainage, and a property that looks finished—not like a construction site.

Fully licensed and insured, focused on doing the job right the first time so your property is ready for the long term.
